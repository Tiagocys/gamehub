Privacy policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

1. Data collected via Google

When signing in with Google, we connect the user's unique ID to the account email and collect, when available:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Profile photo

We do not request access to email content, contacts, Google Drive files or any other additional Google account data.

2. Purpose of data use

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Create and identify the user profile on the platform;
  • Display name and photo on published listings;
  • Enable secure authentication;
  • Prevent fraud and abuse;
  • Improve user experience.

Gimerr does not use Google data for external marketing, spam or sale of information.

3. Data sharing

Gimerr does not sell or trade personal data.

Data may be processed by third parties only when necessary for the technical operation of the platform, such as:

  • Hosting and database services.
  • Authentication platform - Google.
  • Payment processing platform - Stripe. For more information about Gimerr's advertising policy, click here.
  • Messaging app - Telegram, when the user chooses to verify the account via phone number.

These services operate under their own privacy policies and use recognized security standards.

4. Storage and protection

Data is stored in secure infrastructure, with restricted access controls and technical measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration or leakage.

We use encryption and practices compatible with market-standard security measures.

5. Data retention and deletion

User data is kept while the account remains active.

When you delete your account through the platform, all data linked to the profile is automatically and immediately removed from the database.

6. User rights (LGPD)

Under the Brazilian General Data Protection Law (Law No. 13.709/2018), the user may request:

  • Confirmation that data processing exists;
  • Access to stored data;
  • Correction of incomplete or outdated information;
  • Deletion of personal data;
  • Information about sharing.

All information above can be requested via admin@gimerr.com

7. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. In case of relevant changes, the new version will be published on this page.
