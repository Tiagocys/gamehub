Partner policy

Rules for balance calculation, withdrawal requests, and fee deductions applied to payouts.

If you are a game developer, click here and submit it to Gimerr.

1. Dashboard indicators

  • Account: shows the summarized identifier of the payout account registered by the partner.
  • Registered account: indicates whether there is an active account able to receive withdrawals.
  • Balance: shows the gross amount accumulated by the partner from the real consumption of featured listings linked to their servers.
  • Boosted servers: indicates how many servers are currently boosted in the home feed.

2. Calculation basis

  • The advertiser pays for the boost based on the platform's current CPM.
  • The game owner receives 50% of the amount consumed by featured listings linked to their servers.
  • The balance shown to the partner is gross and does not include conversion, transfer, or payout fees.

3. When the balance appears

  • The balance is calculated according to the real consumption of featured listings linked to their servers.
  • Updates may take a few minutes to appear in the dashboard, depending on impression accounting and balance refresh timing.

4. Withdrawal request

  • A withdrawal can only be requested when there is an active payout account and at least R$ 50.00 is available.
  • Before the withdrawal is confirmed, the system shows a preview with:
    estimated fee
    estimated net amount
  • By confirming the withdrawal, the partner agrees that applicable fees will be deducted from the requested gross amount.
  • If the registered account is a Pix key, the request may be processed manually using the key provided by the partner.

5. Fees and conversion

  • Conversion, sending, and transfer fees may vary depending on currency, country, payout account, and requested amount.
  • For bank-account withdrawals, the net amount shown before confirmation is estimated based on the exchange rate and fees returned at request time.
  • For Pix withdrawals, the platform may process the payment manually using the registered key and apply operational adjustments when necessary.

6. Refunds, adjustments, and cancellations

  • If there is any adjustment, reversal, or cancellation affecting listings linked to the partner, the balance may be recalculated.
  • Withdrawals already started may be rejected, cancelled, or adjusted in case of operational or financial inconsistency.
  • When applicable, the partner will be informed through the dashboard or by email about withdrawal status changes.

7. Boosted servers in feed

  • Only the game owner can activate or remove server boosts from the partner area.
  • Boosted servers may receive highlighted positions in the home feed and in the sidebar suggestions.
  • When the same boosted server appears in more than one slot, the system validates at most one billable view per server within each 120-second window in the same browser.
  • If the partner balance reaches zero or a withdrawal enters review, active server boosts may be paused automatically.
  • Boosted servers use the same estimated 7-day CPM tiers as highlighted listings. These values may change over time: 0-25 new listings / 7 days = US$ 0.40; 26-75 = US$ 0.90; 76-150 = US$ 2.00; 151-400 = US$ 4.00; 401-900 = US$ 7.00; 901+ = US$ 12.00.
