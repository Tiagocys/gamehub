Featured advertising policy

Read carefully before adding balance and activating highlights.

1. Adding balance

  • The user adds balance to the ad wallet starting from R$ 5.00.
  • Payment is processed via Stripe in a secure environment.
  • After payment confirmation, the balance becomes available for the user's own listings.

2. Activation and consumption

  • Highlights are activated from the ad wallet dashboard.
  • Highlights follow a billing model based exclusively on CPM.
  • The reference CPM is US$ 2.00 per 1,000 impressions tracked by the system.
  • The BRL amount is converted daily based on the USD/BRL rate used by the system.
  • An impression is counted only when the featured card reaches at least 65% visibility on screen.
  • The same listing can only be counted again after 30 seconds in the same browser tab.
  • Impressions are grouped by the system with a small technical delay of up to 800ms.
  • The 30-second rule is applied in the browser itself to reduce repeated counts of the same listing in the same tab.
  • Tracked impressions are charged in real time based on CPM.
  • When the balance reaches zero, active highlights are paused automatically.

3. Pausing and deleting a listing

  • When a listing is paused or deleted, its highlight ends immediately.
  • Amounts already consumed by impressions are not refunded.
  • Any remaining balance stays in the ad wallet for future use.

4. Withdrawal and balance refunds

  • You may request withdrawal of the available ad wallet balance starting from R$ 5.00.
  • When a withdrawal is requested, active highlights are paused and the balance goes into review.
  • Refund requests below R$ 5.00 are not accepted.
  • The final refunded amount may be lower than the requested balance because original payment fees and other applicable costs may be deducted during refund review.

5. Moderation

  • Listings may be removed for violating the platform rules.
  • Even in moderation removals, consumption already recorded up to the removal time is not reverted.

6. Support

Questions about payments, balance and highlights: admin@gimerr.com

7. Payment security

Payments are processed exclusively by Stripe, in a secure and encrypted environment.

Gimerr does not store sensitive credit card data, such as card number, security code (CVV) or expiration date.

All financial information is handled directly by Stripe, in accordance with applicable international security standards.
