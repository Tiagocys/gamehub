Featured advertising policy

Read carefully before adding balance and activating highlights.

1. Adding balance

  • The user adds balance to the ad wallet starting from the local equivalent of US$ 2.00.
  • Adding balance requires a verified phone number.
  • The ad-wallet checkout currency is chosen automatically from the user's verified phone country, with USD as fallback.
  • The minimum deposit amount for the ad wallet is the local equivalent of US$ 2.00.
  • Payment is processed via Stripe in a secure environment.

2. Wallet currency and activation

  • After payment confirmation, the deposit is credited to the ad wallet. The dashboard may display the most recent deposit currency for convenience.
  • The CPM of each highlighted listing is estimated according to the number of new active listings created for that game in the last 7 days and may change over time.
  • The available ad-wallet balance is used to feature listings and cover CPM consumption.
  • Listing highlights are activated from the listings dashboard.
  • Listing highlights follow a billing model based exclusively on CPM. Current estimated reference tiers:
    0-25 new listings / 7 days: US$ 0.40
    26-75: US$ 0.90
    76-150: US$ 2.00
    151-400: US$ 4.00
    401-900: US$ 7.00
    901+: US$ 12.00
  • Checkout and minimum amounts follow the automatic currency chosen for the user. CPM tiers use USD as the billing reference.
  • When the balance reaches zero, active highlights may be paused automatically.

3. Impression counting and consumption

  • An impression is counted only when the featured card reaches at least 65% visibility on screen.
  • The same listing can only be counted again after 120 seconds in the same browser.
  • Impressions are grouped by the system with a small technical delay of up to 800ms.
  • Only highlighted listing cards that actually appear to the user can generate billable impressions.
  • Refreshing the page or reopening the same screen within the 120-second window does not create a new charge for the same listing in the same browser.
  • Validated impressions are charged in real time using the tier active for that game at the moment of counting.

4. Pausing, deleting, and refunds

  • When a listing is paused or deleted, its highlight ends immediately.
  • Amounts already consumed by impressions are not refunded.
  • Any remaining balance stays in the ad wallet for future use.
  • You may request withdrawal of the available ad wallet balance starting from US$ 2.00, the same minimum amount required to add balance.
  • When a withdrawal is requested, active highlights are paused and the balance goes into review.
  • The final refunded amount may be lower than the requested balance because original payment fees, conversion, and other applicable costs may be deducted during refund review.

5. Moderation

  • Listings may be removed for violating the platform rules.
  • Even in moderation removals, consumption already recorded up to the removal time is not reverted.

6. Support

Questions about payments, balance and highlights: admin@gimerr.com

7. Payment security

Payments are processed exclusively by Stripe, in a secure and encrypted environment.

Gimerr does not store sensitive credit card data, such as card number, security code (CVV) or expiration date.

All financial information is handled directly by Stripe, in accordance with applicable international security standards.
